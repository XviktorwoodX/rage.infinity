R.A.G.E Infinity Development.

VERY active development of R.A.G.E Infinity has now begun, and I am very happy about it! Development was in dev hell for a long time, a lot of things were redone. I divided the entire development into 3 phases, which I will describe now. 

1st Phase it was November 2022, I decided to make models for another mod that has nothing to do with IV. There I was taught to make models with pixel accuracy, and looking at R.A.G.E, I realized that I had not achieved what I wanted. The UVs were still shitty, to put it mildly, they were held together by crutches, so to speak, so I decided to try to make a Datsun 100a model, which I had been making for... 4 months. But the efforts were not in vain, I learned a lot, but I could not find the right style, so the car looked like some kind of candle, but nevertheless, I showed you all sorts of DEV videos, which, by the way, are still relevant (because the ideas that were there are still the same) 

2nd Phase It was time to rework the style, I redrawn the Datsun 100a, making it look like the OCP style, it turned out to be something... similar? However, it was already better than what it was, but there were still some problems. For example, I noticed that on some models the shading looked strange due to the large number of non-standard faces, which made possibly strange shapes, however, I then made a teaser trailer, because I thought that this was the final style (as usual, I decided to burn all the bridges and remake it from scratch). 

3rd Phase Finally the final phase, and now I am completely confident in this style. I took OCP quality style. The cars became a little simpler in style and with a resolution of 16x16, except for some elements in the interior and the car badge. The idea remained the same, just the models and approach to them were refreshed.
