ABOU​T

R.A.G.E Infinity is a pack for Immersive Vehicles that adds a large number of vehicles that you can customize. Do you want to make a sports car out of your car or dismantle it into parts? No problem, you can do it! Just like in R.A.G.E, but with more functionality and better features!

In R.A.G.E Infinity, you can edit any car however you like! Cust​omize it, add body kits, change the interior, and upgrade the engine.

ONLY YO​​UR IMAGINATION

STYLE

You get an eye-pleasing style designed by MrMulle, which is originally used in the Official Content Pack and fits perfectly into Vanilla Minecraft. The models are simple and fit perfectly into the game. Additionally, each car has a real 1:1 size, but if you want your cars to be larger and adapt to other packs' sizes, you can easily increase the cars in Config without any changes!

A LOT OF VEHICLES

In R.A.G.E Infinity, you can find a lot of types of cars, starting with cheap small car and ending with rare hypercar!

PERFECT WITH OCP

You can use the wheels and ornaments from the Official Content Pack in R.A.G.E Infinity, which will perfectly fit into your game and add even more variety!

The wheels in R.A.G.E are fixed at size 1.0, which means that the large wheel can be installed on R.A.G.E Infinity vehicles!

OCP PARTNER

R.A.G.E Infinity, uses quality OCP particles, effects, sounds and style!
