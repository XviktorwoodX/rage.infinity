R.A.G.E Infinity is a pack that adds a large number of vehicles that you can customize. Do you want to make a sports car out of your car or dismantle it into parts? No problem, you can do it! Only your imagination limits you. This pack has a lot of customization, for example, you can start your car by pressing the key in the interior. You can disassemble the engine into 3 parts and change the exhaust pipe. You can disassemble the interior or replace it with another one that you like. You can upgrade the engine, change the appearance, install tinted windows, and customize the tires to your liking, just like in R.A.G.E, but with more functionality and better features!
